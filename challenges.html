1. Push-ups

Benefits: Builds upper body strength (chest, shoulders, triceps), engages your core, improves posture.

  1. Start in a high plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulders.
  2. Keep your body straight from head to heels.
  3. Tighten core and glutes to stabilize posture.

2. Squats

Benefits: Strengthens legs and glutes, improves mobility and posture, boosts balance.

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Keep your chest lifted and spine neutral.
  3. Push your hips back and lower down as if sitting.
  4. Go down until thighs are parallel to the ground.
  5. Don't let knees cave inward.
  6. Keep heels flat and weight distributed evenly.
  7. Engage your core for balance.
  8. Push through your heels to rise.
  9. Exhale on the way up, inhale on the way down.

4. Lunges

Benefits: Builds leg strength, improves balance, isolates each leg for symmetry.
