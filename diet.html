Essential Nutrients

Protein

Builds muscles, repairs tissues, and supports hormones. Sources include eggs, chicken, tofu, and legumes.

Carbohydrates

Main energy source for workouts. Choose whole grains, fruits, and vegetables over refined sugar.

Fats

Supports brain function and energy. Healthy fats are found in nuts, seeds, olive oil, and avocados.

Vitamins

Boost immunity and energy metabolism. Get them from fruits, veggies, and whole foods.

Water

Hydration is key for all body functions. Aim for 8+ glasses a day, more if you exercise regularly.

Lifestyle Tips

Sleep Hygiene

7–9 hours of sleep helps recovery, fat loss, and mental clarity. Set a routine and avoid screens before bed.

Stress Management

Practice meditation, deep breathing, or journaling to manage daily stress for better health and focus.

Healthy Routine

Plan meals, exercise regularly, and limit junk food. Consistency over intensity leads to lasting change.

Screen Time

Reduce excess screen time and stay physically active. Take breaks every hour if sitting long hours.
